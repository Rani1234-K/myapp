Monsoons (July to October) bring some relief from the scorching heat of summers. Day temperatures remain mild around 30°Cand nights become pleasant between 20°C to 22°C. But don’t forget to carry your rain gear,if you plan to visit Pune during this time as the downpour of rain could get messy. The monsoon lasts from June to October, with moderate rainfall and temperatures ranging from 22 to 28 °C (72 to 82 °F). Most of the 722 mm (28.43 in) of annual rainfall in the city falls between June and September, and July is the wettest month of the year. Hailstorms are not unheard of.
